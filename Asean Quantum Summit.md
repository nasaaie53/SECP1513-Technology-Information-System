## Asean Quantum Summit 2025

### 11 December 2025 at Dewan Sultan Iskandar,UTM Johor Bahru

---
### Introduction 
- This event aims to introduce and dedicated advancement in quantum technologies.In order to share the journey into a new technological paradigm.All the researchers,scientist and industry leaders from across Asean and Japan are invited

---
### Contents
- Fields such quantum computer,quantum communication and quantum sensor.These kind of fields are the foundation of technologies that need to be discuss for the bright future of scientific and industry
- Qubit,which is the most basis of Quantum Computer.Qubit is likely to normal bit in computer,but has an abilty to superposition.One of the advantages is can solve complex calculation faster than a normal computer.
- Quantum technology exhibition and opportunities for companies and researchers to showcase their work, as well as MoUs (official collaborations) between universities and companies from ASEAN and Japan
