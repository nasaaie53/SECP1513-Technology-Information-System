## JCorp Industry Visit

### 7 January 2026 at Menara Komtar,Johor Bahru Centre

---

### Summary of the visit

The visit to such a great company was very exciting.Our visit begin with a tour of different rooms and departments in the office.Then,we continue with a breakfast before enter the talk session.The sharing session with Budiman Bujang provided insight into how AI is being used to drive the speed and efficiency of systems in his company. He also explained the technology they use to provide fast response, effective monitoring and assist in decision making.

Moreover,he also shared about the digital strategy which focuses on the use of AI, Automation, and Analytics technologies to empower the three main elements of the organization, namely People, Process, and Innovation. Through the integration of these technologies, the organization aims to continuously increase business value towards becoming a more efficient, synergistic, and productive Exponential Organization (ExO). Overall, this technology is the backbone in ensuring every workflow runs more smoothly and systematically.

---

### Reflection

As a data engineering student who has seen this strategy firsthand in the industry, I realize that digital transformation is not just about software sophistication, but how technology acts as a catalyst to humanize work processes. Furthermore, I see that technical skills need to go hand in hand with the ability to adapt to the digital ecosystem. Integrating AI and Analytics is no longer an option but a necessity to ensure organizations remain relevant, where every decision must be data-driven to minimize risk and maximize impact on society.
