### Content
---
- The System Development Life Cycle (SDLC) consists of several interconnected phases that provide a structured framework for developing a system. These phases        include Planning, Analysis, Design, Development, Testing, Implementation, and Maintenance. Each phase plays a vital role in ensuring the system is successfully     developed, with System Design being especially critical in shaping the final product
- Each phase in SDLC plays an important role and related to each other
- The design phase is the most important phase because the decision is made from here.This phase will affect the quality,efficiency and the system success
- Reduce the dependence on Artificial Intelligence.AI may help us to improve the efficiency,time spent,creativity and solve complex problems.
- The down side of AI can affect our life in terms of problem solving, creativity, and crictical thinking skills.

### Reflection

Attending industry talks made me realize that an IT career is not just about coding, but also about project and system management skills.
